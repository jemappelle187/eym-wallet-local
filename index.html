Market Overview

Stablecoin Advantage

Using USDC and EURC stablecoins, we minimize currency conversion losses and offer beneficiaries a stronger local currency equivalent. EURC appeals to the European diaspora, leveraging the euro's strength to enhance purchasing power and trust.

Scalable & Inclusive

Solana's blockchain enables high-volume, real-time settlement with near-zero fees. Our platform supports both individuals and businesses, integrating with local mobile money providers for seamless access and liquidity.

Global Reach

Send money to 150+ countries with competitive exchange rates and transparent fees. Our platform connects families and businesses worldwide with fast, secure, and affordable cross-border transfers.

Why Choose SendNReceive?

EYM Wallet redefines remittances with instant, fee-free transfers for individuals and low-cost business payments. Leveraging Solana's high throughput and near-zero fees ($0.00025/tx), our app supports high-volume micropayments and real-time settlement. Users can save in stablecoins to avoid inflation, access virtual credit cards, and make online or in-store payments. We integrate with Ghana's Mobile Money Payment Interoperability System for broad accessibility and liquidity.
