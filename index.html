Market Overview

Ghana & Africa Focus

Launching in Ghana's $4.7B remittance market, EYM Wallet aims to expand across Africa, including Nigeria, Uganda, South Africa, and Zambia. Our app addresses the need for fast, affordable, and secure cross-border payments for individuals and businesses.

Stablecoin Advantage

Using USDC and EURC stablecoins, we minimize currency conversion losses and offer beneficiaries a stronger local currency equivalent. EURC appeals to the European diaspora, leveraging the euro's strength to enhance purchasing power and trust.

Scalable & Inclusive

Solana's blockchain enables high-volume, real-time settlement with near-zero fees. Our platform supports both individuals and businesses, integrating with local mobile money providers for seamless access and liquidity.

Why Blockchain Remittances?

EYM Wallet redefines remittances with instant, fee-free transfers for individuals and low-cost business payments. Users can save in stablecoins to avoid inflation, access virtual credit cards, and make online or in-store payments. Our educational content in local languages empowers users to make informed financial decisions.

Security & Compliance

Trust Through Technology

Our app employs cutting-edge encryption, biometric authentication, and real-time fraud detection. Strategic partnerships with Fincra, Utila, and Orca Fraud ensure regulatory compliance and operational security. We are actively pursuing all necessary remittance licenses, leveraging our partners' expertise to navigate the regulatory landscape.

User-Friendly Experience

Our intuitive interface makes digital transactions accessible for all users. From seamless onboarding to visually appealing design, EYM Wallet ensures a smooth experience for sending, receiving, and managing funds.

Innovation in Technology

Leveraging Solana's high throughput and near-zero fees ($0.00025/tx), our app supports high-volume micropayments and real-time settlement. We integrate with Ghana's Mobile Money Payment Interoperability System for broad accessibility and liquidity.

Integration with Daily Life

EYM Wallet seamlessly integrates with local mobile money providers like MTN Ghana, Airtel, and Vodafone, enabling instant payments, business transactions, and everyday purchases. Our platform supports the local economy by increasing liquidity and purchasing power.

Growth & Financial Targets

Our goal: $100,000 annual turnover within 12–18 months (from Jan 2026), capturing 1.12% of Ghana's digital remittance market by 2028. We project profitability by Year 2, with revenues of $333,600 and costs of $114,000, driving sustainable growth and scalability.
